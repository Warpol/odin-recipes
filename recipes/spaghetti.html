Spaghetti

“This ultimate pasta recipe is a total classic, delicious, and super-easy to knock together. ”

The Bolognese Sauce is rich, thick and has beautiful depth of flavour.

It’s perfect for a quick midweek meal but even better if you can simmer it for a couple of hours!

Ingredients

  • onion
  • olive oil
  • dried spaghetti
  • minced beef
  • cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Saute garlic and onion - about 3 minutes
  2. Brown the beef - -about 2 minutes
  3. Add everything else, give it a good stir then simmer for 20 minutes
